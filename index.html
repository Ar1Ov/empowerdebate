Welcome to Empower Debate, where youth voices rise and equity drives every argument

We believe debate is more than a competition — it’s a platform for change. At Empower, students from all backgrounds step into leadership, mastering the tools to challenge injustice, speak truth to power, and uplift one another. From first-time speakers to national champions, every journey begins with a shared belief: your voice matters.
